This extension closes tabs after they have been inactive for a specified amount of time.

The timer resets whenever you use/focus a tab or when the tab is pinned.

You can protect a tab from being closed by clicking the "Protect Tab" button.

You can view the history of closed tabs by clicking the "Expired Tabs History" button.
